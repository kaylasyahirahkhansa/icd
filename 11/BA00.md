# BA00 Essential hypertension

`Code: BA00`

## Description

Essential (primary) hypertension, accounting for 95% of all cases of hypertension, is defined as high blood pressure for which a secondary cause cannot be found.

## Inclusions

High blood pressure.

Exclusions

Cerebrovascular diseases(8B00-8B2Z)

Background retinopathy and retinal vascular changes [9B78.1](/11/BA03.md)
